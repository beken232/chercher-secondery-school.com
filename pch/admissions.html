Application Process

Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements

  • Completion of primary education
  • Minimum grade point average of 3.0
  • Good conduct certificate from previous school

Required Documents

  • Birth certificate
  • Academic transcripts
  • Medical certificate
  • Passport-sized photos
